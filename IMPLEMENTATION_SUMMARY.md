## FAQ

**Q: Can I publish faster than 5-7 articles/day?**
A: Yes, but spreading them out helps with:
- Search engine freshness signals
- Better crawl budget usage
- Time to build proper internal linking
- Gradual domain authority growth

**Q: Should I wait to import all articles?**
A: No, import all at once but schedule them. Publishing in draft allows time for:
- Link building preparation
- Category structure optimization
- Internal linking setup
- SEO configuration

**Q: How do I handle internal links after import?**
A: Two options:
1. Manual review (2-3 hours with 10 people)
2. Automated linking plugins (Yoast, Rank Math, etc.)
3. Natural linking as you write future content

**Q: What if my CMS doesn't support markdown?**
A: Use the conversion scripts in cms-import-guide.md to convert markdown to HTML or your CMS format.
