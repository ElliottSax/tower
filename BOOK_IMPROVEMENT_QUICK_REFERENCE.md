## HELPFUL REMINDERS

1. **Quality over speed** - Takes longer to do it right than to do it fast
2. **Document as you go** - Don't try to remember changes after the fact
3. **Get feedback early** - Don't wait until book is complete to show work
4. **Reuse templates** - Create once, use for all similar books
5. **Celebrate quick wins** - They compound into large improvements
6. **Stay organized** - Use TRACKER, keep file versions clear
7. **Ask for help** - Better to ask than to deliver substandard work
