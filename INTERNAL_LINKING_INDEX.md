### 📊 Scenario 5: "I'm Monitoring Progress (Week 8)"
**Time Available:** 1 hour per week

1. Check INTERNAL_LINKING_IMPLEMENTATION_CHECKLIST.md Phase 5 section

3. Use Semrush/GSC to track 5 pillar keywords
4. Document in spreadsheet
5. Note any improvements (even small ones!)

**Monthly (2 hours):**
1. Review anchor text distribution
   - Sample 30 recent links
   - Calculate % by anchor type
   - Compare to targets (15% exact, 25% partial, 30% branded, 30% natural)
   - Note any imbalances

2. Analyze traffic impact
   - Compare cluster traffic to baseline
   - Look at traffic by article
   - Identify winners and laggards
   - Plan content gaps

3. Check conversion funnel
   - Measure: article → signup
   - Measure: article → affiliate click
   - Track conversion rate by article type
   - Calculate revenue impact
