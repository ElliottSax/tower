## QUALITY SCORE DEFINITION (75/100)

### What Constitutes 75+ Quality:
- ✅ Authentic human voice (not AI-sounding)
- ✅ Real examples and case studies (not generic)
- ✅ Clear structure with logical flow
- ✅ Practical, actionable advice
- ✅ Compelling opening hook
- ✅ Strong conclusion with takeaways
- ✅ Professional formatting
- ✅ Appropriate length (18K-35K words for books)
- ✅ No repetition or weak writing
- ✅ Genre-appropriate tone

### Quality Tiers:
- **90-100**: Publishable, professional, compelling
- **75-89**: Good, ready for market, minor polish possible
- **60-74**: Mediocre, needs work, fixable issues
- **40-59**: Poor, significant rewriting needed
- **<40**: Very poor, consider scrapping or major overhaul
