# Key Principles

1. **Start with free channels** (Medium, LinkedIn, Reddit, Quora)
   - They're infinite ROI (time only, no cost)
   - Most of your traffic will come from here

2. **Build email list obsessively** (Month 1 target: 1,000+ subs)
   - Email is your most valuable asset
   - Email = recurring revenue opportunity
   - Organic traffic = one-time visitors

3. **Optimize for virality** (Reddit guides, HARO mentions, podcasts)
   - One viral post = 1,000+ referrals
   - Don't need all traffic to be "perfect"

4. **Authority = compounding traffic** (Guest posts, backlinks, media mentions)
   - Each backlink = passive traffic forever
   - Each media mention = credibility boost
   - These compound over months/years

5. **Don't wait for organic SEO** (Use syndication + paid ads to bootstrap)
   - SEO takes 6-12 months
   - Your niche has proven demand
   - Speed to market matters more

# FAQ

**Q: Do I need to spend money on ads?**
A: No, but $500-1,000/month in Month 1-2 accelerates growth significantly. You can hit $20K/month with zero ad spend, just takes longer.

**Q: How much time do I need to commit?**
A: 20 hours/week minimum (still achievable), 40-50 hours/week recommended for faster growth. You can batch content creation to reduce daily time.

**Q: When do I start making money?**
A: Month 2-3 you'll see first conversions ($100-500). Month 3+ becomes materially significant ($1,500+). By Month 6 it's a real business.

**Q: What if I don't have an email list yet?**
A: Building the list IS the first 4-6 weeks of work. By end of Week 4 you should have 1,000+ subs. This is your foundation for revenue.

**Q: Can I do this for all 4 sites in parallel?**
A: Yes, in fact it's better because you can batch content (one Medium article that works for all 4 sites is easier). Time scales sublinearly.

**Q: What's the biggest risk?**
A: Doing nothing. Organic SEO takes 12-24 months. These channels get you there in 6 months while also building authority that makes future content rank better.
