Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from Grata or Sourcescrub?

They give you raw company data - you still do all the work. We deliver qualified leads with retirement signals + we do the outreach for you. They're a tool, we're a service.

What if the leads don't convert?

We deliver leads + outreach. Closing is your job. But our success fee alignment (Enterprise tier) means we win when you win. Average conversion: 2-4 deals per 100 outreaches. Even if our conversion matches traditional (it's better), you'll find your deal faster because we feed you 3-4x more qualified conversations.

How do you find owners before they list?

Proprietary retirement scoring algorithm. We analyze: owner age, business tenure, digital decay, website staleness, industry patterns. Score 70+ = high retirement likelihood. Then we test interest with personalized outreach.

Is the outreach spam?

No. We use Claude AI to generate unique, personalized messages. CAN-SPAM compliant with unsubscribe links. Professional sender reputation. 8-12% reply rate proves it's working (spam gets <1%).

How long until I see results?

Week 1: Leads delivered. Week 2: Outreach starts. Week 3-4: First responses. Month 2-3: First meetings. Month 3-6: First LOI. Most pilot clients get 6-9 interested conversations in the first 30 days.

What industries do you cover?

All service businesses, light manufacturing, B2B SaaS, healthcare, professional services, home services. Sweet spot: $500K-$50M revenue. We can source in any geography (50 states + Canada).
