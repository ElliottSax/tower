### If We Don't Hit 2 Pilots by Day 30:

**Diagnosis:**
- Not enough outreach volume? ‚Üí Increase to 100 emails/week

- Pitch not resonating? ‚Üí Refine messaging, test new angles
- Product not ready? ‚Üí Offer free trials, collect feedback

**Action:**
- Double outreach volume (100 ‚Üí 200 emails/week)
- Offer free month 1 (just pay for results)
- Pivot messaging: "No-risk trial: If you don't get 5 conversations in 30 days, free"

---

### If Pilots Don't See Results:

**Diagnosis:**
- Leads are bad quality? ‚Üí Refine scoring algorithm
- Outreach not getting replies? ‚Üí A/B test messaging
- Wrong industries? ‚Üí Pivot to higher-response verticals

**Action:**
- Over-deliver: 200 leads instead of 100
- Personalize every outreach manually (vs. AI)
- Offer full refund + free extra month
- Fix before scaling to more clients
