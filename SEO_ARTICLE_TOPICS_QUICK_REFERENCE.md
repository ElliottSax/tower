## Keyword Research Validation Checklist

Before writing each article:

- [ ] Search volume: 300-5,000 searches/month (sweet spot)
- [ ] Competition: "Easy" to "Medium" (Ahrefs/SEMrush)
- [ ] Intent match: Does article meet search intent?
- [ ] Internal linking: 3-5 existing articles to link to?
- [ ] Unique angle: Different from top 3 SERP results?
- [ ] Affiliate potential: Clear monetization path?
- [ ] User benefit: Solves specific problem?

## SEO Best Practices Per Article

### Structure
- **Title**: 50-60 characters, primary keyword, benefit-driven
- **H1**: Same as title
- **H2-H4**: Secondary keywords, logical flow
- **Paragraphs**: 2-4 sentences max
- **Bullet points**: For lists, comparisons

### Length
- **Minimum**: 2,400 words
- **Target**: 2,800-3,500 words
- **Maximum**: 5,000+ (only for comprehensive guides)

### Links
- **Internal**: 3-5 links per article (to other site articles)
- **External**: 2-3 authority links (Wikipedia, gov, research)
- **Affiliate**: 2-4 affiliate links (natural, helpful)

### Media
- **Images**: 3-5 per article (optimized, alt text)
- **Infographics**: 1 per 2,500 words (complex info)
- **Videos**: Optional (YouTube embeds for tutorials)

### Meta
- **Meta description**: 155-160 characters, compelling CTA
- **Slug**: Lowercase, hyphens, keyword-focused
- **Category**: Proper taxonomy
- **Tags**: 5-8 relevant tags
