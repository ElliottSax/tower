## QUALITY METRICS & TARGETS

### Scoring Methodology
Each book evaluated on 6 dimensions:
1. **Authenticity/Voice** (0-10): How real and credible is the author?
2. **Content Depth** (0-10): How thoroughly are topics explored?
3. **Examples/Specificity** (0-10): How detailed and real are the examples?
4. **Production Quality** (0-10): How professionally formatted/designed?
5. **Reader Experience** (0-10): How engaging and actionable?
6. **Market Positioning** (0-10): How well positioned for target audience?

**Final Score**: Average of all 6 dimensions × 10

### Current State Analysis
- **Elliott Books**: Strong in production/experience (8.5 avg), weak in authenticity/depth (8.0 avg)
- **Books/Book**: Weak in authenticity (6.8 avg), moderate in everything else (7.1 avg)
- **BookCLI**: Critically weak in voice (5.5 avg), acceptable in structure (6.5 avg)

### Target State
- **Elliott**: All dimensions 8.5+, especially authenticity and depth
- **Books/Book**: All dimensions 8.0+, with clear differentiation from Elliott
- **BookCLI**: Voice 8.0+, depth 7.5+, creating authentic expert positioning
