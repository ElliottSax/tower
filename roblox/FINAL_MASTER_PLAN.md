**Reason:** Obby market declining, no differentiation, Tower of Hell dominates

**Reason:** 5 fatal flaws in head-to-head racing discovered:
1. Competitive games harm friendships (increase toxicity)
2. Network latency creates unfairness
3. Procedural generation = RNG imbalance

5. CPU limits with 6-player instances

**Final Model:** Tower of Hell mechanics + skill-based meta-progression
