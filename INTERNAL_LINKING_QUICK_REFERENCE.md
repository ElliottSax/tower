# Internal Linking Quick Reference Guide
## For Content Teams - Fast Implementation Reference

**Updated:** March 2026
**For:** Content writers, editors, and coordinators

---

## TL;DR: The 3-Link Rule

Every article should have AT LEAST these 3 internal links:

```
1. PILLAR LINK (Introduction or "Related" section)
   └─ Link to the main topic article (exact match anchor)
   └─ Example: "For a complete [email marketing tools] comparison, see..."

2. RELATED LINK (Within cluster)
   └─ Link to 1-2 related articles in the same topic area
   └─ Example: "Also read about [ConvertKit vs Mailchimp]..."

3. NEXT STEP LINK (Call-to-action section)
   └─ Link to a beginner guide or strategy article
   └─ Example: "Ready to get started? See our [email marketing for beginners] guide"
```

That's it. Just 3 links. Most articles should have 3-5 total.

```

"Check out our [best email marketing tools] comparison for all options"
```

### For Product Reviews
```
"[Product Name] offers..." (branded)
"See our [Product Name] review for full details" (branded)
"Check out [Product Name] if you need..." (branded)
```

### For Comparisons
```
"For a detailed comparison, see our [Product A vs Product B] article"
"You can [compare Product A and Product B] side-by-side"
"Need help deciding? Read our [comparison of the two]"
```

### For Guides/Hubs
```
"Learn [email marketing] fundamentals in our beginner's guide"
"For an introduction to [the topic], check our guide"
"Starting out? See our [topic] for beginners guide"
```

### For Strategy Articles
```
"Follow our [strategy topic] framework to..."
"Read our guide on [maximizing rewards] for tips"
"For advanced tactics, see our [strategy] article"
```

## Where to Put Links (By Section Type)

| Section | # Links | Anchor Type | Example |
|---------|---------|------------|---------|
| **Intro/Hook** | 1 | Exact match | "See our [pillar article] for context" |
| **Background/Context** | 1-2 | Natural | "As we discussed in our [related article]..." |
| **Main Content** | 1-3 | Branded | "Check the [product] review for details" |
| **Comparison Table** | 3-5 | Branded | Link each product row to its review |
| **Pros/Cons Section** | 1-2 | Natural | "For more on this, read our [strategy guide]" |
| **FAQ Section** | 1 | Natural | "See our [comparison] for this discussion" |
| **Call-to-Action** | 1 | Natural | "Ready to start? See our [beginner guide]" |
| **Further Reading** | 2-3 | Varied | List related articles at bottom |

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Too Many Links**
- ❌ Adding 15+ internal links to one article
- ✅ Keep it to 3-8 links max

❌ **Irrelevant Links**
- ❌ Linking email tools article to credit cards
- ✅ Keep links within same topic cluster

❌ **Poor Anchor Text**
- ❌ "Click here" or "Read this"
- ✅ Use descriptive anchor text with keywords

❌ **All Exact Match**
- ❌ 80% of anchors as "best email marketing tools"
- ✅ Mix: 15% exact, 25% partial, 30% branded, 30% natural

❌ **Forced Links**
- ❌ "For more on unrelated topics, see..."
- ✅ Only link when contextually relevant

❌ **Same Anchor Every Time**
- ❌ Using same anchor text 5+ times
- ✅ Vary anchor text across articles

❌ **Link in Wrong Section**
- ❌ Intro should flow; don't jam links in first sentence
- ✅ Link naturally where content mentions related topic

## Article Type Linking Templates

### Review Articles (1,000-1,500 words)
```
Structure:
├─ Introduction
│  └─ Add link to PILLAR article (exact match)
├─ Overview section
│  └─ Add link to BEGINNER GUIDE (natural)
├─ Features section
│  └─ (No links required)
├─ Pricing section
│  └─ (No links required)
├─ Pros/Cons section
│  └─ Add link to ALTERNATIVE product (1-2 links, branded)
└─ Conclusion
   └─ Add link to COMPARISON or STRATEGY article

Total: 3-4 internal links
```

### Comparison Articles (1,200-1,800 words)
```
Structure:
├─ Introduction
│  └─ Add link to PILLAR article (exact match)
├─ Product A Deep Dive
│  └─ Add link to Product A REVIEW (branded)
├─ Product B Deep Dive
│  └─ Add link to Product B REVIEW (branded)
├─ Head-to-Head Comparison
│  └─ (No links)
├─ Pros/Cons by Use Case
│  └─ Link to STRATEGY guide (natural, 1-2 links)
└─ Conclusion
   └─ Add link to BEGINNER GUIDE or NEXT STEP

Total: 4-6 internal links
```

### Beginner Guide/Hub Articles (1,500-2,200 words)
```
Structure:
├─ Introduction
│  └─ (Hook the reader, no link needed)
├─ What is [Topic]? Section
│  └─ Add link to PILLAR article (exact match)
├─ Why [Topic] Matters? Section
│  └─ (No links)
├─ Top [3-5] Tools/Options Section
│  └─ Add links to each REVIEW (branded, 3-5 links)
├─ How to Get Started Section
│  └─ Add links to STRATEGY articles (natural, 1-2 links)
├─ FAQ Section
│  └─ Add link to COMPARISON if relevant (natural)
└─ Next Steps Section
   └─ Add links to ADVANCED articles (natural, 1-2 links)

Total: 6-8 internal links
```

### Strategy/Advanced Articles (1,500-2,000 words)
```
Structure:
├─ Introduction
│  └─ Add link to PILLAR (exact match)
├─ Background/Context
│  └─ Add link to BEGINNER GUIDE (natural)
├─ Strategy Deep Dive (multiple sections)
│  └─ Add links to relevant TOOLS/PRODUCTS (1-3 links)
├─ Implementation Steps
│  └─ Link to SPECIFIC TOOL REVIEWS (branded, 1-2 links)
├─ Common Mistakes
│  └─ (No links)
└─ Conclusion
   └─ Link to NEXT LEVEL strategy (natural)

Total: 4-6 internal links
```

## Linking Checklist (Before Publishing)

For EVERY article you publish or update:

- [ ] **Pillar Link Added?**
  - [ ] Pillar article identified
  - [ ] Link added in intro or context section
  - [ ] Anchor text is exact match or natural
  - [ ] Link relevant and contextual

- [ ] **Related Link Added?**
  - [ ] 1-2 related articles identified in cluster
  - [ ] Links added in main content
  - [ ] Anchor text is branded or natural
  - [ ] Links make contextual sense

- [ ] **CTA/Next Step Link Added?**
  - [ ] Next step article identified
  - [ ] Link added in conclusion or CTA section
  - [ ] Anchor uses natural language
  - [ ] Directs user's journey forward

- [ ] **Link Quality Check**
  - [ ] All links are clickable/working
  - [ ] No broken links
  - [ ] URLs are correct
  - [ ] Links open in correct way (new tab? same window?)

- [ ] **Anchor Text Variety**
  - [ ] Not using same anchor text >1 time
  - [ ] Mix of anchor types (branded, natural, exact, partial)
  - [ ] Anchors are descriptive
  - [ ] No keyword stuffing in anchors

- [ ] **Linking Appropriateness**
  - [ ] All links are relevant
  - [ ] Not over-linking (max 8 links)
  - [ ] Links flow naturally in text
  - [ ] No forced/awkward linking

- [ ] **Cluster Consistency**
  - [ ] Linking to other cluster articles
  - [ ] Not linking outside cluster (unless strategic)
  - [ ] Linking pattern matches cluster structure
  - [ ] Follows site's linking style

## What To Do When...

### "I'm writing about a tool not in our cluster"
✅ **DO:** Link to the closest related pillar article
✅ **DO:** Link to a beginner guide if available
❌ **DON'T:** Force an irrelevant link

### "I found an old article with no internal links"
✅ **DO:** Add it to the linking update queue
✅ **DO:** Add minimum 3 links (pillar + related + cta)
✅ **DO:** Refresh date if content still current
❌ **DON'T:** Publish without adding any internal links

### "An article I'm linking to doesn't exist yet"
✅ **DO:** Create it or mark as "to be created"
✅ **DO:** Plan the link placement anyway
✅ **DO:** Add the link once article is published
❌ **DON'T:** Publish a broken link

### "I want to add more links to boost SEO"
✅ **DO:** Add up to 8 links if content supports it
✅ **DO:** Ensure all links are relevant
✅ **DO:** Vary anchor text
❌ **DON'T:** Add links just for sake of adding them
❌ **DON'T:** Exceed 8 internal links per article

### "The best link anchor text is 5+ words"
✅ **DO:** Use 3-5 word anchors (that's OK!)
✅ **DO:** Make sure it's still natural-sounding
❌ **DON'T:** Make anchors overly long/awkward

## FAQ: Common Questions

**Q: How many internal links should an article have?**
A: 3-8 links is ideal. Start with at least 3 (pillar + related + cta).

**Q: What's the best anchor text?**
A: Depends on the link. Use variety: 50% natural language, 30% branded, 15-20% exact/partial match.

**Q: Should I link to competitors?**
A: Only within comparison articles, and only when making a direct comparison. Keep most links within your own content.

**Q: Do internal links help with rankings?**
A: Yes! They pass "link juice" and help Google understand relationships between pages.

**Q: Should links open in new tab?**
A: Usually no. Keep visitors on your site. New tab only for external links.

**Q: Is there a penalty for too many internal links?**
A: No penalty, but too many can overwhelm readers. 3-8 is optimal.

**Q: Should I update old articles with new links?**
A: Yes! Updating internal links can give old articles a ranking boost.

**Q: Can I link to pages outside my cluster?**
A: Sparingly. Strategic cross-cluster links are good (2-3 per article max).

**Q: Should I mention the linked article in the anchor text?**
A: Yes if possible. "See our best email marketing tools comparison" is better than "click here."

**Q: Do internal links hurt if they're not perfectly relevant?**
A: Not really, but they don't help either. Keep all links relevant.
